Who we are...

...Graduate students from the Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Sciences (RSMAS). We are passionate about climate science and each of us has dedicated themselves to answering questions about it during their PhDs. We would like to share our knowledge with the community in a variety of outreach programs.

Outreach

Teaching and inspiring children and adults alike using interactive presentations. Presentations are between 30 and 45 minutes, and can cover a variety of topics (see some examples here). These presentations can be on- or off-site, as well as electronically delivered. Contact us for more information.
